How it works

Up and running in 3 steps

No technical skills needed. You'll be handling emails with AI in under 10 minutes.

1
Connect your Gmail
Click "Connect Gmail" and sign in with your Google account. ReplyMate only requests the permissions it needs — read and send emails on your behalf.
2
Set your preferences
Tell ReplyMate your name, how you like to write, your availability, and anything the AI should always mention or always avoid. Takes 3 minutes.
3
Let it handle your inbox
Open your inbox, tap "Generate reply" on any email, review it, and hit send — or turn on auto-send to have replies go out automatically. You're done.
